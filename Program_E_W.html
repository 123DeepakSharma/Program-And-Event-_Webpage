About the Progrm

CSRBOX, in collaboration with IBM SkillsBuild, brings you the Project Based Learning Program 2025 — a 4-week hands-on learning experience designed for students across India. Choose from three exciting themes: Data Analytics, Front-End Web Development, or Agentic AI. Each program is packed with expert guidance, real-world tools, and collaborative team projects (4–8 members per team). Students can enroll in only one program. Registration is absolutely free, with a deadline of 20th May 2025. Don't miss this opportunity to build, learn, and shine on a national platform!
